Mad Scientist

A mad scientist bio-engineers a plant that feeds off sins as a form of purifying the world and releases it into Las Vegas, Nevada.

Exponential Growth

Not very long before the plant grows towards the city.

State of Emergency

A state of emergency is declared and sinning tourests and residents of Las Vegas are forced to evacuate.

Not on my watch

F-22s are deployed by the US Government to exterminate the target at all costs.

Not a chance

The bombing only feeds the plant as it continues to grow and devour the city.

What to do?

After returning to assess the disaster, the Zappos llama is spoted feeding on the bio-engineered plant. Giving the US Government an idea.

Andes Mountains

Black Hawks are deployed to South America in an effort to bring back heards of llamas, their ability to scale almost any terain and their apetite for plants makes them the perfect candidate.

Not on my watch 2.

The llamas become the last line of defense and are safely deployed over Las Vegas in a final attempt to restore the sin.

Nom Nom Nom

About

Hello Zappos! My name is Angel Jimenez and I study Computer Science at California State University Fullerton. My passion for technology and the wheels that make our Internet world turn grows stronger every day. I am excited to be a Front-End Developer because I am an artist at heart and there is no better way for me to express to millions of people my vision than through there interactive experience. The following are a couple of technologies I dabbled with over the weekend to create my Llama inspired app:

HTML5

HTML is the backbone of web pages.

CSS3

CSS was used to dress up my website to format the layout and render images.

Twitter Bootstrap

I incorporated Bootstrap into my design because it provides great functionality for devices of all sizes. It also gave me the chance to incorporate jQuery to implement some javascript functionality like the random quote generator.

A nice web font is critical to the feel of a website, so this gave me the opportunity to inject my website with Google's API which provides hundreds of great fonts to use.
